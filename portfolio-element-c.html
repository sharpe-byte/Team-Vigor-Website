Element C: Presentation & Justification of Solution Design

Customer Requirements

Based on the results our customers gave us on the survey:

  • 52.4% preferred to have a slim case
  • 63.1% requested to have a more durable phone
  • Customers wanted to have a phone that's cheap to repair based on how 60.7% are willing to pay under $100 to fix their damaged device.
  • Customers wanted to have a more effective tempered glass or a stronger screen for their phones based on how 58% of the them drop their phones in a week and how 51.8% them are willing to replace the screen of their phone once there device had been damaged.

Design/Product Specifications

Our group has chosen to take the top three requested problems that were concluded in our Phone Durability Survey:

  • Slim housing
  • A more durable phone
  • More effective tempered glass
